Refund Policy
CarWashMi only charges the customer upon completion of services by Car Care Professional ( CCP). In case of any complaints contact customer service to file a complaint.
Cancellation fees are charged to compensate CCP for time invested in traveling to a user's location.
When a CCP is en route to your location, you can use your app to cancel your ride request. Your account may be charged a cancellation fee. This depends on how much time has passed between when the driver accepted your request and when you cancel.
CCPs are also able to cancel a request. In this case, you may be charged a cancellation fee if the driver has already waited for several minutes at your given location.
Cancellation fees may differ depending on your city. Many cities have a 5 minute charge-free cancellation window. In other cities, you may be charged a fee for cancelling more than 2 minutes after a CCP is en route to your location.
